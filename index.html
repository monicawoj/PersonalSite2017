Business and Technology

I'm a senior in the Jerome Fisher Program in Management and Technology at Penn studying Systems Engineering and Decision Processes (Wharton). Systems engineering utilizes engineering methodologies to optimize the interaction between interdependent entities. Decision Processes aims to explain why such systems tend to fail when we add human behavior and biases to the picture.

America and Poland

As a first-generation American, I am entirely grateful to be able to call two countries "home sweet home". My love for Poland goes well past the pierogi and kielbasa. My Polish heritage has shaped my values, ideals, and perspectives. My American home has provided me with the opportunities, friends, and mentors for them to blossom and grow.
