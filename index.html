About

I thrive when worlds collide - marketing and engineering, data and art, computation and conversations. I seek to provide value and gain experience across functional areas and disciplines. I enjoy a good challenge and, as an engineer by nature, am always looking to introduce systematic improvements and innovations to my surroundings.

Business and Technology

I'm a proud graduate of the UPenn Jerome Fisher Program in Management and Technology, through which I obtained a BSE in Systems Engineering and a BSE in Operations, Information, and Decisions (Wharton). As an engineer, I enjoy order and the hum of an efficient and effective system. As a business professional, I enjoy ensuring that hum harmonizes with its human stakeholders.

America and Poland

As a first-generation American, I am entirely grateful to be able to call two countries "home sweet home". My love for Poland goes well past the pierogi and kielbasa. My Polish heritage has shaped my values, ideals, and perspectives. My American home has provided me with the opportunities, friends, and mentors for those ambitions to blossom and grow. I hope to serve as (self-appointed) Polish-American goodwill ambassador for life.

Data and Art

To put it simply, I want to be the next bestselling author- with one caveat: I want to tell stories through numbers and visuals, not just words. Creating a visually attractive and valid representation of data has the potential to inform an audience, change behavior, combat perceptions, and ultimately, improve the world’s systems it has saught to ellucidate.
